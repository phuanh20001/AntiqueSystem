The Process
How AntiqChain Works
Four steps from submission to blockchain-sealed certificate.
01
Submit Your Item
Upload high-resolution photos, provenance documents, and detailed information about your antique through our secure portal.
02
Expert Review
A certified verifier examines your submission, cross-references historical records, and assigns an authenticity score with a written assessment.
03
Blockchain Sealing
Upon approval, the verification data is hashed using SHA-256 and permanently recorded in our tamper-proof blockchain ledger.
04
Certificate Issued
Receive a digital certificate of authenticity bearing your item's unique blockchain hash — valid for resale, insurance, and estate records.
